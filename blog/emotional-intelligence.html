Emotional Intelligence.

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to percieve, interpret, demonstrate, control, evaluate and use emotions to constructively and effectively communicate and relate to others, people suggest that emotional intelligence is more important than the general IQ. EQ can help manage your own emotions in postive ways to help relieve stress, communicate effectively and overcome challenges. Emotional Intelligence helps you build stronger relationships, succeed more, and achieve goals.

How is EQ different to IQ?

The difference between EQ and IQ is that IQ tests and measures your ability to solve problems, use logic and communicate complex ideas whereas EQ tests and measures your ability to recognize emotions within yourself and others.

Why is EQ important?

A high Emotional Intelligence helps your to build relationships, remove tension, reduce teams stress levels, defuse conlicts and it also highly imrpoves yoour job satisfaction.
